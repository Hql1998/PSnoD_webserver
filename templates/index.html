PSnoD

Predictor for snoRNA association with Disease

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Many studies have evidenced that small nucleolar RNAs (snoRNAs) play critical roles in the development of various human complex disease. Discovering the associations between snoRNAs and diseases is an important step for understanding the pathogenesis and characteristics of diseases.

This study adopted a bounded nuclear norm regularization (BNNR)-based method to predict snoRNA-disease associations. The benchmark dataset is filtered from Mammalian ncRNA-Disease Repository (MNDR v3.1), After removing entries with redundant or missing information, 220 snoRNAs, 28 diseases, and 459 known associations between the two were adopted as benchmark dataset.

You could use this tool to predict potential disease associations with your intrested snoRNA.
